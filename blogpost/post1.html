Blog 1: The CIA Triad of Cybersecurity!

Introduction

What is Cybersecurity? Cybersecurity is alway a big focus that everyone talk about in today tech age. From big tech company to the Department of Defense, cyber security is alway the big concern; However, how do we know if our data is safely secure and threat free? Today, I will explore the three main principles/requirement that must be met so we can safely say that our system is cyber secured. We can call these three priciples as the CIA Triad of Cybersecurity:

  • Confidentiality
  • Integrity
  • Availability

Confidentiality

What is it mean by confidentiality? It is the property of information is not made available or disclosed to unauthorized individuals. The ability to keep a piece of infornmation or data a secret and maintain it's secrecy from unauthorized access is the ability to keep data confidential. Lack of confidentiality will lead to leakage of data and can result in millions of damage or even national security.
