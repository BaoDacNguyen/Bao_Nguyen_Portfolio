Blog 1: The CIA Triad of Cybersecurity!

Introduction

What is Cybersecurity? Cybersecurity is alway a big focus that everyone talk about in today tech age. From big tech company to the Department of Defense, cyber security is alway the big concern; However, how do we know if our data is safely secure and threat free? Today, I will explore the three main principles/requirement that must be met so we can safely say that our system is cyber secured. We can call these three priciples as the CIA Triad of Cybersecurity:

  • Confidentiality
  • Integrity
  • Availability

Confidentiality

What is it mean by confidentiality? It is the property of information is not made available or disclosed to unauthorized individuals. The ability to keep a piece of infornmation or data a secret and maintain it's secrecy from unauthorized access is the ability to keep data confidential. Lack of confidentiality will lead to leakage of data and can result in millions of damage or even national security. Preventions method should be implement to help keep authorized users safe and keep out unathorized users. End users is often the biggest weakness in the security chain so we should focus more user's training. In addition to training, common security authentication method is two-factor authentication along with biometric verification, security tokens and digital certificates.

Integrity

Integrity in cybersecurity is the ability to prevent unauthorized modification of information. Any unauthorized changes will be in violation of the integrity of that piece of information. Unauthorized alteration doesn't neccessary mean that it come from an intruder but also from unintentional alter by flaw in the system or users. A prevention method to secure intergrity is strong authentication method that only allow authorized users to make changes and not by anyone else. To accomplish that, we can assign digital certificates/tokens to users and implements a secure transport protocol.

Availability

In today age of high speed internet, our daily tasks require a large amount of information readily available to us in a reasonably fast amount of time. To ensure availability, there should be steps that protect the data from failures and also antivirus software to prevent malwares from corrupting the server. One type of attack that targeted availability specifically is Denial-of-Services attack.

Conclusion

In conclusion, by maintaining the three principles of cybersecurity, we guarantee to protect our data and server from unauthorized access. Each principles relies on each others to provide full protection so by uphold one principles it help us maintaining the other principles as well. Good practices would be strong authentication methods with access control in place. Security measures in cybersecurity are build on these basic principles and prevent attacks that attempted to violate these principles.
